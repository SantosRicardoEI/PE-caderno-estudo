Considere a v.a. discreta $X$ com função de distribuição

$$F(x) = \begin{cases} 0, & x < 0 \\ \tfrac{1}{6}, & 0 \le x < 2 \\ \tfrac{1}{4}, & 2 \le x < 4 \\ \tfrac{1}{2}, & 4 \le x < 6 \\ 1, & x \ge 6 \end{cases}$$

**(a)** Determine a função de probabilidade de $X$.

**(b)** Calcule: **i.** $P(X \le 1)$; **ii.** $P(X > 5)$; **iii.** $P(0 < X \le 2)$; **iv.** $P(2 \le X < 6)$.

## Solução

**(a)** A f.p. são os saltos de $F$, nos pontos $0, 2, 4, 6$:

$$P(X=0)=\tfrac16, \quad P(X=2)=\tfrac14-\tfrac16=\tfrac{1}{12}, \quad P(X=4)=\tfrac12-\tfrac14=\tfrac14, \quad P(X=6)=1-\tfrac12=\tfrac12.$$

**(b)** Lendo diretamente de $F$ (lembra que $F(x)=P(X\le x)$):

- **i.** $P(X \le 1) = F(1) = \boxed{\tfrac16}$.
- **ii.** $P(X > 5) = 1 - F(5) = 1 - \tfrac12 = \boxed{\tfrac12}$.
- **iii.** $P(0 < X \le 2) = F(2) - F(0) = \tfrac14 - \tfrac16 = \boxed{\tfrac{1}{12}}$ (só o valor $X=2$).
- **iv.** $P(2 \le X < 6) = P(X=2) + P(X=4) = \tfrac{1}{12} + \tfrac14 = \boxed{\tfrac13}$.
